1. Eutropia Branding

2015.11.20

A branding system I developed around a chapter from Italo Calvino's book, Invisible Cities. The system exists as a set of rules and is applied in both print and spatial branding exercises. I later built a responsive website to house the project.

2. Soph Branding

2015.08.05

My most recent itteration of a dynamic / responsive identity using a 3D symbol set. Applications include miscellaneous print collateral, packaging, adaptive exhibition design, 3D animation, a short publication, and a responsive website to house the project.

3. Overview of a Work in Progress

2015.04.12

A research initiative I started mid January with eight of my peers, resulting in a small publication and group show. A second iteration of the project is currently under way.

4. Surface No. 1

2014.12.19

A translation of a short piece of fiction I wrote into a non-linear story telling app. The app simplifies setting into a single orbiting system of symbols. The apps functionality is shown here through a promotional animation.

5. Synthetics Branding

2014.12.14

My first exploration of dynamic identity systems using a 3D symbol set. Applications include print, wearables, and web-design.
